Font Sizes
Three font sizes are available, use classes 'text-xl', 'text-lg', 'text-md', 'text-sm', 'text-xs' for extra large, large, medium, small and extra-small text.

  <div class="text-xs">Extra Small Text</div>
Center Text
To create center text, use 'text-center' class.
Lorem ipsum dolor sit, amet consectetur adipisicing elit. Iusto asperiores officia impedit placeat excepturi aperiam quia, quam nam, ut eius ratione dolorum, consequuntur architecto debitis ullam ipsa quod soluta culpa!
