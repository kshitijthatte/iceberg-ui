Rating
Rating shows the opinion of others and their experience with the product.
Filled Ratings
To create filled rating component, use the 'ratings' class. For the icons here 'material-icons' are used.
star star star star_outline star_outline 
                
  <div class="ratings material-icons">
      star star star star_outline star_outline
  </div>
Rating Badge
To create a smaller rating component, use the 'rating-badge' class.
4.1 star 
                
  <div class="rating-badge">
      4.1 <span class="material-icons">star</span>
  </div>
