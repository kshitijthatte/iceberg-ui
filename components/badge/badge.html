Badge
The Badge component superscripts or subscripts an avatar-like icon to highlight information to a user. Content within the badge usually contains numbers or icons.
Badge on Avatars
The badge component can be used by applying 'badge' class and putting that div inside a div with the 'badge-container' class. The bagde can further be customized using 'bg-green' or 'bg-red' class. 
                
<div class="badge-container">
  <img class="avatar" src="/assets/avatar/1.jfif" alt="" />
  <div class="badge"></div>
</div>
<div class="badge-container">
  <img class="avatar" src="/assets/avatar/2.jfif" alt="" />
  <div class="badge bg-green"></div>
</div>
<div class="badge-container">
  <img class="avatar" src="/assets/avatar/3.jfif" alt="" />
  <div class="badge bg-red"></div>
</div>
Badge on Icons
To use the badge with icons as a notification badge, place the notification count number inside the 'badge' div.
8
2
                  
<div class="badge-container">
  <img class="icon" src="/assets/icons/mail_icon.svg" alt="" />
  <div class="badge">8</div>
</div>
<div class="badge-container">
  <img class="icon" src="/assets/icons/shopping-cart_icon.svg" alt="" />
  <div class="badge bg-red">2</div>
</div>
Bottom Badge
Position of the badge can be changed to bottom-right by using the 'badge-b' class, along with 'badge' class. 
                  
  <div class="badge-container">
    <img class="avatar" src="/assets/avatar/1.jfif" alt="" />
    <div class="badge badge-b bg-green"></div>
  </div>
Badge Sizes
The Badge component comes in 3 sizes. It can be changed by adding 'badge-sm | badge-md | badge-lg' class to the existing 'badge' class.
2
                  

<div class="badge-container">
  <img class="avatar" src="/assets/avatar/1.jfif" alt="" />
  <div class="badge badge-b bg-green badge-sm"></div>
</div>
<div class="badge-container">
  <img class="avatar" src="/assets/avatar/1.jfif" alt="" />
  <div class="badge badge-md"></div>
</div>
<div class="badge-container">
  <img class="icon" src="/assets/icons/shopping-cart_icon.svg" alt="" />
  <div class="badge bg-red badge-lg">2</div>
</div>
