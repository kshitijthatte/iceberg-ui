Grid
The grid creates visual consistency between layouts while allowing flexibility across a wide variety of designs.
Two column grid
To create a two column grid, use the 'grid-col-2' class along with the 'grid' class on the parent container of grid items.
1 of 2
2 of 2
                
  <div class="grid grid-col-2">
    <div class="grid-item">1 of 2</div>
    <div class="grid-item">2 of 2</div>
  </div>
