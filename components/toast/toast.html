Toast
Toasts are a non-disruptive message in the botttom of the interface. They provides quick 'at-a-glance' feedback on the outcome of an action.
Standard Toast
To create a standard toast use the 'toast' class. Then use the 'toast-label' and 'toast-actions' classes for toast label and to wrap action buttons.
Can't send photo. Retry in 5 seconds. 
                
  <div class="toast">
      <div class="toast-label">
        Can't send photo. Retry in 5 seconds.
      </div>
      <div class="toast-actions">
        <button class="btn btn-transparent btn-sm">Retry</button>
        <button class="btn btn-transparent btn-sm btn-icon">
          <span class="material-icons">close</span>
        </button>
      </div>
  </div>
